Suspicious Story

It all started on a quiet day aboard the Skeld. Red was in Admin, definitely not faking card swipe for the 5th time. Green was in MedBay scanning... a sandwich? Blue claimed they were doing tasks, but the only task they were doing was watching TikToks in Navigation.

Then — bam! Lights out. Classic. Everyone panicked. Someone screamed “IT’S YELLOW!” but there was no Yellow. There never was. A ghost? A glitch? Or just someone forgetting their colors?

An emergency meeting was called. No one had evidence, just vibes. Pink was ejected. They were not the impostor. Oops.

In the end, it turned out the ship was haunted by the ghost of a rejected crewmate... who just wanted to finish their tasks in peace.
